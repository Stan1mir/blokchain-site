Solana (SOL)

Built for Speed: A High-Performance Blockchain

Solana is a "Blockchain 3.0" platform designed from the ground up to solve one of the most critical issues plaguing older blockchains: speed. It is built to be a high-performance network capable of supporting decentralized applications that require massive transaction throughput, like high-frequency trading or global-scale games.

Core Features: High Throughput and Low Costs

Solana achieves its incredible speed—capable of processing up to 65,000 transactions per second (TPS)—through a unique innovation called "Proof of History" (PoH). This mechanism works alongside a highly efficient consensus model to create a verifiable order of events without requiring all nodes to communicate constantly. This positions Solana as a powerful platform for demanding applications that need to operate at a global scale, without the high costs and slow speeds associated with older networks.
